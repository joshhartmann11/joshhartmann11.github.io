Who am I?

Besides building things, I enjoy backpacking, biking, skateboarding, volleyball, skiing/snowboarding, and friends.


What is this website for?

It was suggested to me by my co-op advisor to keep track of the projects I do on co-op as part of a profile. I thought there would be no better way to show my projects than with another project. So that is this. As well, I've always wanted to keep track of the progress I make on things I build, so keeping a project blog of sorts is the perfect solution.


Web dev?

I became jealous and, of course, wanted to do it better, so I googled how to make a website. I spent hours learning HTML, CSS, and a little JS, only to learn that my brother made his website using www.freewebs.com. The skills stuck but I have little interest in front-end web development.
