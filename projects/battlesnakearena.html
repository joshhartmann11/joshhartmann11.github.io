I was wanting a local battlesnake arena for testing battlejake 2.0 so I made one. The arena allows for:
  • Skipping the graphics and rapidly running games allowing for faster conversion for genetic algorithms and for a fast reference of the fitness of a snake
  • Custom arena sizing, food abundance, and number of snakes
  • Customization of the game mechanics

Slight modifications need to be made to snakes to convert them from a server to something that doesn't need any networking overhead but I modified a couple of the past winning snakes for testing mine.
