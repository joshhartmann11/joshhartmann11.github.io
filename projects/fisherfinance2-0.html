The new Fisher Finance 2.0 is built as a strict framework for supporting plug and play tools, rapid development, and sharing your homemade tools! Will I ever finish? maybe, but I'm learning lots along the way!


The goal of Fisher Finance 2.0 is for it to run 24-7 on a low powered computer, such as a raspberry pi, acting as a local server. This will allow for the monitoring of information without a burden to everyday work flow on a personal machine. The framework will allow for homemade tools to be plugged in and shared with export/import tool options. The tools will have access to obj common database of common company and stock information and GUI controlls and objects.
