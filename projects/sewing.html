A while back, my mom got a new sewing machine and I decided to take her old one to school. I've been sparsely using it for about a year, mostly for fixing pants, but also for making a few things, here are some of my projects.


The computer case on the left was the first item I made on the machine, and was an abrupt introduction to zippers. The case on the right is for my girlfriend's new laptop. Both are made from old pants, t-shirts, and zippers salvaged from an old bag.

Someone told me that I couldn't make a quilt, so I did. It made for a great christmas present and so did the pencil cases!
